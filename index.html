Deploy MON-20 Token

Initialize the MONS token inscription with the following parameters. This can only be done once.

Inscription: data:,{"p":"mon-20","op":"deploy","tick":"mons","max":"21000000","lim":"100","mlim":"100"}

Mint MONS Tokens

Each mint creates 100 MONS tokens. Fee: 1 MON per mint. Maximum 100 mints per wallet.

Inscription: data:,{"p":"mon-20","op":"mint","tick":"mons","amt":"100"}

⚠️ Make sure you have enough MON in your wallet to cover the mint fee (1 MON) plus gas fees.

Transfer MONS Tokens

Transfer your MONS tokens to another address. Make sure the recipient address is correct.

Inscription: data:,{"p":"mon-20","op":"transfer","tick":"mons","to":"0x...","amt":"100"}
